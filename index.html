Bacon Omelette

Ingredient Amount
Egg 3 pcs
Onion 1 pcs
Bacon 200 g
  1. Chop the onion into pieces of a suitable size for you and fry on a pan. Fry the bacon togehter with the onion.
  2. Whisk the eggs lightly, add salt and pepper.
  3. When the bacon and onion are lightly browned, pour the egg into the pan over the bacon and onion. Cook over low heat until the omelet has solidified.
